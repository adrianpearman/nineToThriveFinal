About 9 to Thrive

9 to Thrive was created out of the 9 to 5 grind.

Founder, Sarah Beatty, was constantly struggling to balance a busy workload with her health and well-being.

Inspired by her passion for health and wellness, she focused on creating an easy way to bring healthy activities TO the workplace. It's a win-win!

The 9 to Thrive team has years of corporate event management experience and worked with a throughly- vetted network of the best health and wellness experts in the city.

We know exactly how to get an event started in your workplace! From start to finish, are here to support you through every phase of launching culture initiatives that support healthy habits.
